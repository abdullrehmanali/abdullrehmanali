About Me

Hi, I'm Abdul Rehman Ali! I am a Mechanical Engineer and MSc student at Aalto University, Finland, with a strong interest in mechatronics, automation, and innovative product development.

My journey in engineering began when I disassembled my first remote-controlled car at age 10. Since then, my curiosity has only grown stronger, leading me to pursue a career where I can create solutions that positively impact our planet.
